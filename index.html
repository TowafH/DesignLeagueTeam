About This Project

Recycle House is a game that addresses the issue that many places around the world do not take seriously. As we see every day, many people throw out their trash and leave it wherever they want. This is very harmful to the environment in several different ways. To tackle this, we asked ourselves, how can we change people's minds and help them understand the uses of the things they throw on the ground? So we created this game.
